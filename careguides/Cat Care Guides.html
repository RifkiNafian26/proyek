Cat Care
Guides

Approved Meals & Tasty Treats

Make sure to feed your cat a high-quality diet suitable for their age, size, and health needs, provide clean fresh water every day, avoid harmful human foods like chocolate, onions, garlic, and dairy products, and offer treats only in moderation to prevent weight gain and health problems.

Chasing, Climbing & Curious Playtime

Help your cat stay active and mentally stimulated by offering daily play with toys like feather wands, laser pointers, or toy mice, provide scratching posts, climbing spaces, or cat trees, and create safe enrichment activities that allow your cat to exercise, explore, and express natural hunting instincts.

Soft Fur & Trimmed Claws

Keep your cat clean and comfortable by brushing their fur regularly (especially for long-haired breeds), trimming their nails when needed, cleaning their ears if necessary, and scooping the litter box daily while fully changing the litter on a routine schedule to maintain a fresh environment.

Safe Spaces, Healthy Habits & Vet Care

Protect your cat's wellbeing by scheduling regular veterinary visits, keeping vaccinations and parasite preventions up to date, using gentle positive reinforcement during training, offering a calm and cozy resting area, and keeping dangerous objects like toxic plants out of reach.
